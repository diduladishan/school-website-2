Welcome to School Life at Dar Al Marefa

We approach school life holistically. We focus on fostering a positive emotional state amongst students just as much as we push for academic and educational excellence.

Student well-being is an essential element and a determining factor in a child’s overall success, in school and out. At Dar Al Marefa, everything from our teaching methods to the architecture and layout to the school plays a key role in supporting students’ mental health and well-being.
