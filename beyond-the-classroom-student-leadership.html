Student Leadership at Dar Al Marefa

At JBS, leadership is a key part of our educational approach, encouraging students to develop character, confidence, and a sense of purpose. Through formal leadership roles, enrichment programs, and after-school activities, students learn to lead with integrity, work collaboratively, and contribute meaningfully to the school and wider community.

Inspiring Leaders at
Dar Al Marefa

Empowering students through meaningful leadership opportunities.

Head Boy and Head Girl

Open to Grade 12 students, these roles involve representing the student body, chairing councils, and leading initiatives that promote school values. Head Boy and Head Girl also collaborate closely with staff to enhance student engagement, organize key events, and serve as role models for their peers.
