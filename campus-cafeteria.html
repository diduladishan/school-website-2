CAFETERIA

INTRODUCTION

At Dar Al Marefa, we believe healthy eating habits start at school. Our cafeteria provides students with fresh, locally sourced meals that are both nutritious and affordable. By combining taste, variety, and health-conscious preparation, we ensure students enjoy their meals while learning about the importance of making the right food choices.

BEYOND MEALS

A LEARNING EXPERIENCE

The nutritional lessons students receive in class are brought to life in our cafeteria, where food becomes an extension of their learning journey. Each meal reflects our commitment to well-being and supports students in developing positive lifestyle habits.

Provide an enjoyable, nutritious, and attractively presented selection of food and drinks at reasonable prices

Help reduce health risks by encouraging good eating habits

Offer practical experiences for students to make informed, healthy food choices

Maintain high standards of hygiene in preparation, storage, and serving

Empowering curious learners through bilingual
(Arabic & English), inquiry-driven education.
