Overview

Our state-of-the-art campus has been designed and built from the ground up to maximise students’ potential for learning. The visionary campus was designed by the globally-awarded RCR architects, winners of the Pritzker Laureate prestigious architecture award, often referred to as the Nobel Prize of Architecture. The school architecture and design stimulate discovery, learning, growth and creativity, for students of all ages. Shadows, colours and shapes meet to form intricate patterns and pathways all around the campus, to generate a lively and cheerful environment.

Our Facilities

Our facilities reflect our aim of providing our pupils with a broad and balanced IB Curriculum educational and communal experience, and include:

  • An extremely well-resourced library building
  • Healthy food choices canteen building
  • Double volume air-conditioned sports hall
  • Multi-purpose PE studios
  • Temperature-controlled swimming pool
  • Large floodlit sports field
  • Two outdoor tennis courts
  • 400-seat auditorium building
  • Eight science labs
  • Fully equipped ICT, art & design and music studios
  • Extensive shaded walkways throughout the entire campus
  • Well-equipped playground areas
