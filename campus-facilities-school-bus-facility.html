SCHOOL BUS TRANSPORT

Dar Al Marefa provides a secure and efficient bus service for students who require transport to and from school. Our services is managed in partnership with the RTA to ensure comfort, safety, and timely arrivals. Parents are encouraged to coordinate with our RTA representatives to arrange their child's pick-up and drop-off schedule.

MEETING THE BUS

  • Be accompanied by a parent/guardian to and from the designated waiting area.
  • Arrive at least five minutes before the regular pick-up time.
  • Avoid play or unsafe activities while waiting.
  • Board the bus in an orderly manner.
