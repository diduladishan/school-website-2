Developing Your
Child’s Universal Skills

A philosophy of education that goes beyond limitations. At JBS, we believe that education extends far beyond academic achievement. Our goal is to equip students with essential life skills that help them thrive in all aspects of life – leadership, communication, wellness, and more – ensuring they grow into well-rounded individuals prepared for the future.

Developing Skills Beyond the
Core Curriculum

Our programmes focus on building essential skills and positive habits that extend learning beyond the classroom. Students are encouraged to practice leadership, collaboration, perseverance, and responsibility, developing the confidence to meet challenges and pursue excellence in everything they do.

ARTS

Open to Grade 12 students, these roles involve representing the student body, chairing councils, and leading initiatives that promote school values. Head Boy and Head Girl also collaborate closely with staff to enhance student engagement, organize key events, and serve as role models for their peers.
