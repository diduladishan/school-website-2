SCHOLARSHIPS

Opportunities for High-Achieving Students in Grades 8–11 – Academic and Sports Scholarships Available

MISSION STATEMENT

To facilitate the education of gifted students, whether current or future, thus promoting an enriched and challenging educational environment that will enhance learning opportunities for all.

APPLICATION PROCESS

Applications are reviewed by the scholarship committee and approved by the Principal. Decisions are final, and the school may modify or withdraw scholarships at any time.

ELIGIBILITY & LENGTH

Scholarships will be awarded for one academic year and are open to students from Grade 8 to Grade 11 for the forthcoming academic year.

WHO CAN APPLY?

Consideration will be given to students who have demonstrated high academic or athletic achievement and have an exemplary school record. To apply please click the Admissions Team link, where you can email our team directly.

SCHOLARSHIP CRITERION & PROGRAM

Dubai Distinguished Students Programme

About the Program

This programme stems from Dubai Social Agenda 33 and is a collaborative effort between the government and the private sector. The programme aims to provide high-quality educational opportunities for outstanding Emirati students at all stages of their education, focusing on those students who have demonstrated academic excellence, leadership potential, and a strong commitment to continuing their education.

To facilitate this, the Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA) and the Knowledge Fund Establishment have signed agreements with a group of elite private schools that offer a diverse range of internationally recognised curricula.

Dubai Distinguished Students Programme

The scholarship covers 50% of the tuition fees for students attending schools rated Good or better, with the remaining 50% to be covered by their parents or legal guardians.
