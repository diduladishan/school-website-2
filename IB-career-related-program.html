Programme Introduction

IB Diploma Programme

The DP is a balanced programme that equips students with rigorous academic skills and real-world readiness. With a focus on holistic education, the DP encourages inquiry, intercultural understanding, and personal reflection. It prepares students not just for university, but for lifelong learning and contribution to society.

Emphasis on research and critical thinking
Global contexts and international-mindedness

The IBDP

LEARNING MODEL

At the heart of the DP is the IB learner profile, which defines the attributes of a responsible, active global citizen. Students engage with six subject groups and core components: Theory of Knowledge (TOK), the Extended Essay (EE), and Creativity, Activity, Service (CAS). This framework promotes intellectual balance and ethical responsibility.
