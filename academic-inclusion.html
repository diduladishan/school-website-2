OUR COMMITMENT TO

INCLUSION

Empowering every student through personalized learning support and inclusive practices.

At Jumeira Baccalaureate School, inclusion is at the heart of our philosophy. We believe that every student deserves access to a high-quality education that supports their unique learning needs. Our inclusive approach ensures that gifted, talented, and students of determination receive tailored support designed to help them thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Early Intervention

And Partnerships

We recognize the importance of early intervention and proactive support to ensure student success. By working in partnership with parents, specialists, and educators, we create a cohesive framework of support. Our team closely monitors student progress and provides targeted resources to enhance learning outcomes while building confidence and independence.

Supporting Students of Determination &

Special Educational Needs (SEN)

Our approach ensures that students with special educational needs receive the right level of support through tailored learning strategies. We adopt an inclusive philosophy that values individual strengths and talents, offering a safe and nurturing environment that celebrates diversity while fostering achievement and growth.

High achievers receive enrichment opportunities.
Gifted & talented students benefit from tailored extension programs.
Students of determination receive specialized support to meet unique learning needs.

English as an Additional

Language (EAL)

With English as the primary medium of instruction, we provide specialized EAL programs to ensure that all students develop fluency and confidence in the language. Our approach includes structured support, immersion opportunities, and individualized learning plans designed to accelerate language acquisition and academic success.

Families
