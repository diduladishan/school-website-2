Applying to Dar Al Marefa Made Simple

A Three-Step Process for Joining Our International Baccalaureate World School

Online Application

All applications are digital and submitted online through our website. We have no paper applications.

In the application, you will be asked for some general information about your child and contact details, and you will be asked to upload some preliminary documents to complete the application.

You can expect to receive a confirmation that we have received and reviewed your application within one to four business days.

However, depending on when you apply, we will only proceed to Step 2 when we know of a possible opening. Additionally, to proceed to the documentation stage you will be required to pay a non-refundable application fee of 525 AED.

Documentation

Please ensure that all required documents are submitted prior to attending your assessment appointment.

Kindly scan and email the documents in advance to admissions@daralmarefa.ae.

Please note that failure to fully disclose information regarding a child’s additional learning needs may put the child’s place at the school at risk.

  • Student Documents
    • Student passport
    • Residence visa
    • Emirates ID
    • Student Photo
    • Birth certificate
  • Parents Documents
    • Parents passports
    • Residence visas
    • Emirates ID
  • School Report & Transfer Certificate
    • Most recent school report for all G2 to G12 applicants (upon assessment)
    • Transfer Certificate if applicable (at the end of the academic year)
  • Medical Records
    • Vaccination Record
    • DHA Medical form (filled at the day of the assessment)
  • Diagnostic testing, psychological or educational assessment (if applicable)
    • The school requires a copy of any Diagnostic testing or educational assessment (if applicable) for your child.
  • DMS conduct for all G1 to G12 applicants (if applicable)
    • For all students from Grade 1 to 12, Dar Al Marefa required the DMS conduct for to be filled from their previous school and sent to admissions@daralmarefa.ae
  • Primary Phase (Pre KG – G1)
    • For Prekg to KG2 & G1 Your child will be invited for an informal play base assessment at the school, either in a group setting or one-on-one. For families based outside of Dubai, alternative arrangements will be made.
  • Primary Phase (Grade 2 – Grade 5)
    • Students applying for Grades 2 to 5 will complete a computerized Cognitive Ability Test (CAT4).
  • MYP-DP (Grade 6 to Grade 12)
    • For Grades 6 to 12, students will undertake a computerized knowledge assessment (Arabic, English, Mathematics, and Science), in addition to the CAT4 and an interview to determine their academic level. We will contact you with the date and time of your child’s assessment, which will be conducted by the school admissions and academic team in Dar Al Marefa campus.

Assessment

If places are available, you will receive an invitation to submit further information and/or schedule an assessment. The assessment process helps us understand the student’s level upon joining the school, enabling our team to support them effectively from the start and ensure they gain the most from their learning experience.

The assessments are designed according to each educational stage and are age-appropriate, allowing us to accurately determine each student’s abilities.

Acceptance and Registration

Following the assessment, and upon receiving an acceptance letter, a registration deposit fee will be required to secure the seat. Please note that a seat cannot be reserved until the deposit payment is received. The deposit amount will be specified in the acceptance letter.
