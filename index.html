Dear Dar Al Marefa family,

Please allow me to introduce myself, my name is Mr. Steven Giles, and I am the principal at Dar Al Marefa School. I am extremely proud to lead this wonderful school community, which is the embodiment of an extended family for us all. From our youngest students entrusted into our excellent KG, all the way through to our eldest students studying to gain entry into the university of their dreams, I am proud of the educational offer every one of our students receive.

I am passionate about ensuring students are happy, confident and empowered to reach new heights, take risks, and challenge themselves. They will be truly nurtured and motivated to learn new skills every single day through an innovative and holistic educational offer. I believe in a reflective, tolerant, and respectful environment for all, widening horizons and setting aspirational targets.
