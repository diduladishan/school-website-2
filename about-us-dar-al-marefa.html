Inspections & Accreditations

Accreditation

As a part of our strategy of continuous improvement and self-evaluation, JBS is proud to have achieved a number of accreditations by independent educational bodies who regularly assess the quality of our teaching and learning, ensuring that we meet and maintain international standards of quality and integrity regarding academics, administration and related services.

Inspection

Like every school in Dubai, we are inspected annually by the Dubai Schools Inspection Bureau (DSIB), part of the Knowledge and Human Development Authority (KHDA)’s Dubai Schools Inspection Bureau (DSIB). The KHDA is responsible for the growth, direction and quality of private education and learning in Dubai.

School of Character

DAM is proud to be one of the only schools in the region that is fully accredited and offers all four International Baccalaureate Programmes; the Primary Years Programme (PYP), the Middle Years Programme (MYP), the Diploma Programme (DP), and the Career-Related Programme (CP).

KiVa

DAM is proud to be the first international Quality Mark school outside the UK to be awarded the Quality Mark status by the Association for Character Education (ACE).
