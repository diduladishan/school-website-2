Dubai International Academy offers a Variety of Co-Curricular Activities

Our co-curricular activities are intended to provide opportunities for all pupils to discover and develop their own talents and interests while building confidence, social skills, and teamwork in a nurturing environment.

Primary School Co-Curricular Activities

Our Primary School CCA offers age-appropriate opportunities that engage curiosity, strengthen collaboration, and support the development of key life skills through meaningful participation.

1 guaranteed CCA per term at no charge.

More CCAs can be signed up for (subject to availability).

Leadership opportunities for Year 5 & 6 students.

Wrap care available for KG students (extra charge).

Secondary School Co-Curricular Activities

Our Secondary School CCA broadens student experience through leadership, service, and specialist enrichment, enabling students to pursue interests while preparing for future pathways.

Activities available for both girls and boys.

Minimum of six sessions per activity each semester.

Each student allowed one activity per semester.

No changes once activities are chosen.

Empowering curious learners through bilingual
(Arabic & English), inquiry-driven education.
