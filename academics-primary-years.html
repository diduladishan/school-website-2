Programme Introduction

IB Primary Years

The PYP learning environment is guided by the principles of inquiry-based learning, where students are inspired to ask questions, seek answers, and develop problem-solving skills. Teachers act as facilitators, encouraging students to think deeply, work collaboratively, and connect their learning to real-world experiences.

A focus on conceptual understanding
Active student engagement in learning processes
Integration of transdisciplinary themes

THE PYP

LEARNING MODEL

The PYP framework combines essential elements—knowledge, concepts, skills, attitudes, and action—to create a balanced learning experience. This model ensures that students not only gain academic understanding but also develop the values and attributes needed to become responsible global citizens.

The PYP at

Dar Al Marefa

A unique approach blending global IB standards with a bilingual learning experience.

At Dar Al Marefa, we implement the PYP with a commitment to cultural diversity and bilingual education. Students learn in both Arabic and English, developing strong communication skills while embracing global perspectives. Our educators provide an inspiring and supportive environment where every child feels valued and motivated to achieve their best.

Empowering curious learners through bilingual
(Arabic & English), inquiry-driven education.
