PASSIONATE ABOUT DAR AL MAREFA

Our parents work as members of the Parent Association, contributing to building a supportive and vibrant school community. We are dedicated groups of volunteers and elected leaders. They work tirelessly to organize events, foster meaningful engagement, and ensure collaboration between students, parents, and staff throughout the year.
